Who We Are

Welcome to Meds Express Pharmacy, a family-owned, multi-service retail and compounding pharmacy proudly serving the Texas healthcare community. As a certified sterile compounding pharmacy, we specialize in preparing both sterile and non-sterile customized medications designed to meet each patient's unique needs.

Our experienced team is dedicated to precision, safety, and quality in every prescription — from everyday medications to highly specialized therapies. We work closely with patients and providers to ensure personalized care that is both effective and reliable.

At Meds Express Pharmacy, we are committed to delivering exceptional service, expert guidance, and competitive pricing. Our pharmacists are always available to answer your questions and support your healthcare journey with professionalism and care.
