Sterile Compounding

Our ISO-certified clean-room facility enables the preparation of sterile products with the highest standards of purity and safety — essential for injectable and ophthalmic medications.

All sterile preparations comply with USP <797> guidelines and undergo rigorous quality checks before dispensing.

  • Injectable preparations
  • IV admixtures & infusions
  • Ophthalmic drops & ointments
  • Nasal sprays
  • Pain management compounds
  • USP <797> compliant processes
